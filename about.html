How I work

Four things that don't change per project.

Story first, footage second

The order things were filmed in is rarely the order they should be watched in. The cut serves the story, not the shot list.

Sound is half the edit

A clean cut with muddy audio still feels unfinished. Dialogue leveling and noise cleanup happen before picture lock, not after.

Color is a mood, not a filter

A grade should match what the footage is trying to say, warm and inviting or cool and serious, rather than get applied the same way every time.

Deadlines are part of the craft

A great edit delivered late isn't useful to a channel with a publishing schedule. Turnaround is planned in from the first ingest.
