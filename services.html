Services

Pick the format. Get an edit built for it.

Every service below can run on its own or stack together, a podcast episode that also needs three short-form clips pulled from it, for example.

01

Long-Form & YouTube Editing

Typical turnaround: 3–5 business days

A full pass on long-form footage: pacing, b-roll placement, jump-cut cleanup, and structure built to hold attention past the intro.

  • Story-driven rough cut and refine pass
  • B-roll and cutaway placement
  • Chapter markers and end screen setup
  • Captions and basic lower thirds
02

Podcast Editing & Production

Typical turnaround: 2–4 business days

Descript-based cleanup and production for interview and solo-format podcasts, video or audio-only.

  • Filler word and dead-air removal
  • Level balancing and noise cleanup
  • Chapter markers and show notes support
  • Clip pulls for social posting
03

Short-Form & Social Cuts

Typical turnaround: 1–2 business days

Vertical reformatting for Reels, TikTok, and Shorts, built around a hook in the first two seconds.

  • 9:16 and 1:1 reframing from source footage
  • Burned-in captions, styled to the brand
  • Trend-aware pacing without losing the message
04

Color Grading & Correction

Typical turnaround: 1–3 business days

DaVinci Resolve grading for a consistent look across cameras, lighting setups, and locations.

  • Base correction for exposure and white balance
  • Creative grade matched to the project's mood
  • Multi-camera match for interview setups
05

Audio Cleanup & Mixing

Typical turnaround: 1–2 business days

Dialogue leveling, background noise reduction, and music ducking so nothing fights the voice.

  • Noise reduction and de-essing
  • Dialogue leveling across takes and mics
  • Music bed mixing under narration
06

Motion Titles & Captions

Typical turnaround: 1–2 business days

Styled captions, lower thirds, and simple motion graphics that support the edit instead of distracting from it.

  • Custom caption styling matched to brand
  • Lower thirds and title cards
  • Light motion graphics for callouts
07

Web Development

TechJex's newest niche scoped per project

Responsive websites, Webflow builds, and internal systems for clients who need a site to go with the edit or just a site.

  • Responsive, brand-ready business websites
  • Webflow builds for fast, no-code-friendly launches
  • PHP/MySQL internal systems and dashboards
